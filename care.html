Plant Care Tips

Learn how to properly care for your outdoor plants!

Outdoor plants need consistent watering, but avoid overwatering. Each plant differs in how often it needs watering, so be sure to read the guide on the individual plant. Keep an eye on the weather as well! Rain can be a friend.

Most plants require at least 6 hours of sunlight a day, but it depends on the plant. Plants are either full-sun, partial-sun, or partial-shade. Read the guide provided on the plant to decide where to plant it.

Plants will not grow without healthy roots, so it is important that plants have the right soil and drainage. Fertilize the soil before planting, and for a few weeks after. Putting rocks at the bottom of the hole can also be helpful with drainage.

Mulch around plants every year to promote drainage and healthy soil. Prune (trim) plants if needed. This promotes growth the next bloom.
Care Checklist
  • Water plants based on soil dryness
  • Ensure the plant gets the right sunlight
  • Use well-draining and fertilized soil
  • Prune regularly for healthy growth
