Popular Plants

Lilac

A fragrant, purple flower that blooms in the spring. It thrives in full sun and moist soil.

Sunlight

Honeysuckle

Honeysuckle is a fast-growing vine with sweet-smelling flowers that attract pollinators.

Water

Wisteria

Wisteria produces cascading purple blooms in spring and needs full sunlight and support to climb.

Care

Plant Details
Lilacs thrive in full sun with moist soil. Honeysuckle grows quickly and benefits from pruning, while wisteria needs strong support structures and regular maintenance.
